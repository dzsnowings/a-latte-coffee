How to Make a Mocha

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces freshly brewed coffee (or instant)
  • 1/2 cup (4 ounces) milk
  • 1 tablespoon (15 g) cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon (15 g) warm water
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Whipped cream and cocoa (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Brew desire about of coffee.
  2. Make a cafe-style chocolate syrup with warm water and sweetened cocoa powder. Combine equal parts of each and stir up in small bowl. Create 2 tablespoons for one drink.
  3. Combine chocolate syrup with coffee.
  4. Steam some milk or heat it on the stovetop or in the microwave.
  5. Fill your mug with heated milk.
  6. Top with whip cream, a sprinkling of cocoa powder, and enjoy!

Congratulations! You made a mocha!
