How to Make a Frappe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups double-strength coffee, chilled
  • 1/2 cup low-fat milk
  • 2 Tbsp. sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups ice, plus more as desired
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, puree coffee, milk, sugar, and ice.
  2. Pour into 2 glasses, and top with whipped cream and chocolate sauce if desired.
  3. Serve immediately.

Congratulations! You made a frappe!
