How to Make a Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1.5 cups strong brewed coffee

Instructions

  1. Combine the sugar and milk, then heat it in your microwave for 2 minutes at the highest setting.
  2. Whip the heated milk/sugar mix in your blender for about one minute or until it becomes a fluffy foam.
  3. Fill your cup 2/3 full with the coffee, and top it off with your heated froth. Serve.

Congratulations! You made a cappuccino!
